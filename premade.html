About premade covers

Serendipity

Do you know that feeling when some fortunate discovery takes you by surprise? Try, browse the library of premade covers and see — perhaps the perfect cover for your book is already out there…

Inspiration

Usually the story comes first, and the cover follows. But where does the story come from? Perhaps the idea for it was sparked by and image. Have a look — can you tell the tales that hide behind these covers?

Economy

Are you just starting out as an author? Do you need a cover but you’re on a tight budget? A premade cover might be just the solution. Sold once and customized to your needs — all at a modest price.

How it works

Have you found a cover you like? Great!

Please tell me which cover you want to buy and whether you need it for an ebook, a print book, or both. Let me know how would you like to customize it (title, author’s name, any other details) and if you need specific cover dimensions.

I will review your order to make sure I can implement all of your requests and get back to you, if I need to clarify anything. Once we’ve got all of that sorted, I will send you a PayPal link which you will be able to use to pay for the cover either with a card or via your own PayPal account.

Sounds good? Get in touch and we’ll take it from there!
