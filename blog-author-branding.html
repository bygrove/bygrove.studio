Author branding

Example: Caimh McDonnell

Book series are a great opportunity for an author to develop their branding. But some writers go beyond the book covers and extend their branding all the way to their websites — which is a great idea.

I have recently discovered Caimh McDonnell and his books. He writes humorous crime thrillers, which is not a genre you often come across. But he definitely pulls it off and how!

Books by Caimh McDonnell

Also, his book covers are brilliant and perfectly reflect his writing. The stark color scheme suggests the fast-paced thrills, while the font and the cartoony style clearly communicate the humor.

Some of the books are funnier, while others are darker, but his writing style is consistent throughout (and yes, I’ve read all of his books and I’m impatiently waiting for more) and — so is his website.

The same color scheme, similarly whimsical fonts (I’m pretty sure it’s Gilligans Island on the covers and Ranchers on the website).

As you can see on the image below, the website and the covers clearly belong together — and together they’re an awesome example of a thorough author branding.

Consistent design throughout the book covers and website - author branding!
