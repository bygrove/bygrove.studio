About me

I love my work

Designing book covers is a lot of fun and I want to make sure that you, too, enjoy the experience. The job of a book cover is to delight and to communicate. I believe the best covers achieve this because they’re born from passion and are tailored through cooperation between the author and the designer.

I’m always learning

Designer’s training is never finished. Every day I acquire new skills, learn exciting graphic techniques, and discover original color schemes. I follow latest trends and actively seek inspiration, every day turning this knowledge into my craft. Every project is a new challenge and with every design I grow as an artist.

I’m a writer too

Therefore, I appreciate what it means to be putting your words out there. I understand the importance of market positioning and discoverability and how both can be aided by a good book cover. As a writer, I walk the indie publishing path and as a designer, I use this experience when crafting book covers.
