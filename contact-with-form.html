About me

I love my work Designing book covers is a lot of fun and I want to make sure that you, too, enjoy the experience. The job of a book cover is to delight and to communicate. I believe the best covers achieve this because they’re born from passion and are tailored through cooperation between the author and the designer.
I’m always learning Designer’s training is never finished. Every day I acquire new skills, learn exciting graphic techniques, and discover original color schemes. I follow latest trends and actively seek inspiration, every day turning this knowledge into my craft. Every project is a new challenge and with every design I grow as an artist.
I’m a writer too I appreciate what it means to be putting your words out there. I understand the importance of brand positioning and discoverability and how both can be aided by a good book cover. As a writer, I walk the indie publishing path; as a designer, I use this experience to craft beautiful book covers that work.

Get in touch

Would you like me to design your cover?

Please tell me a little about your book. Is it fiction or non-fiction? What genre? Do you need an ebook cover, a print book cover, or both? Do you have any ideas for your cover? A particular color scheme, tone, visual style? Feel free to send me examples of book covers you like.

I will get back to you to discuss all further details.

Do you want to buy a premade cover?

Please tell me which cover you’d like, whether you need it for an ebook, a print book, or both, and what elements you’d like customized.
